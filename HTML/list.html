Safiur's Recipe for the Best cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1/4 warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspons yeast
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3 cup bread flour

for the filling :

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablepoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast ,sugar ,eggs
  2. Melt thhe butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined in to a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minuites
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap ,Leave it some where to rise for 2 hours
